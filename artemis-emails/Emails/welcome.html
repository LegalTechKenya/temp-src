What is Artemis?
 
Artemis is a large language model that's designed to provide information on Caselaw, Acts of Parliament, and Subsidiary Legislation, as applies in Kenya.
As a large language model, Artemis is able to hold a conversation in natural language, providing responses to your queries in natural language.
What Can Artemis Do?
Caselaw
You can use Artemis to get detailed information about cases from Kenyan courts of record e.g. the Facts, Issues, Judgement(s) / Ruling(s), Presiding Judge(s), etc.
Legislation
Artemis can provide the relevant provisions of law that deal with a specific subject matter. This includes any provisions that are referenced in Caselaw.
Comparisons
You can also compare cases and outcomes e.g. How many cases of a specific type were presided over by a specific Judge(s) and what were their outcomes?
