Our First Virtual Session

We are set to begin this project on Monday, 15th November 2021. We would love to have an open forum where all the community members can briefly introduce themselves, and we can provide a summary of what this project is, the vision and what success looks like for this project. To this end, we will be sharing a Virtual Meeting link over the weekend.

We have set up a Form for community members to inform us of their availability for the meeting. We will set up the meeting for the most popular time. Please note that we will record all sessions and make these recordings available for community members who may not be able to attend a session.

Project Tools

We will be making use of various software tools for the management of this community and project. For project management, we will be making use of Clickup. This is a web-based service that you can access through your browser on any device. We will be sending out invitations to join the Workspace shortly.

For general and teams communications, we will be making use of Slack. You can download this application for your computer or mobile device Here. We will be sending out invitations to join via email, shortly.

We will be making use of GitHub for collaborative software development and version control. If you are not signed up on GitHub, you can do so Here. Please share a link to your profile via email, so that we can add you to the Organization.

Attached to this email is a summary of the teams and roles for this community. Please have a look at this and decide what team best suits you. You may join as many teams as you wish.
