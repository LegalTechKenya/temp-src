Week Two of the Legal Tech Kenya Open-source Community
Our 2nd Week
For our second week, the focus of the LTK Open-source community was on how community members can make contributions. To this end, there were two virtual meetings held. The first was on Monday 22nd November, 2021. This meeting was primarily for the developers in the community. The members discussed how to make contributions to the software development work of the community.
The second meeting was held on Wednesday 24th November, 2021. This meeting was led Mr. Brian Muriithi, a Commercial and Conveyancing lawyer who is the founding and managing partner of Muriithi Muthomi Associates Chambers. He led the members through the legal requirements necessary for a successful implementation of the project. These include: Substantive laws on land ownership; Procedural laws; The conveyancing process; and Documentation requirements.

Both sessions were recorded and are available on Youtube for members who were not able to attend.
