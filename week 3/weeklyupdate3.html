Week Three of the Legal Tech Kenya Open-source Community Project
Our 3rd Week
For our third week, the focus of our community has been on getting the teams up and running. The community is currently divided into the Legal Team and the Developers Team. For each of these teams, various tasks have been set up on Clickup. Community members are free to pick up and work on tasks at their convenience.

Tasks available for the Legal Team are as follows:
  1. Substantive Laws - A comprehensive listing of all substantive land laws
  2. Procedural Laws - A comprehensive listing of all procedural laws on transactions in land
  3. Documentation Requirements - A comprehensive listing of all documentation requirements for land transactions
  4. Software Requirements Specifications - A comprehensive description of the system requirements

Tasks available for the Developers Team are as follows:
  • Frontend Developers
    1. Wireframes
    2. Mockups
    3. UI Prototyping
    4. Flowcharts
  • Backend Developers
    1. Authentication and Authorisation Microservices
    2. Abstract Base Models
    3. Notification Microservices
    4. Error Logging
    5. Request-response and application logging
    6. Asynchronous Communication
    7. Cron Jobs
